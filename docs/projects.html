RSSI Positioning System

I'm currently working on developing a WiFi RSSI positioning system as part of my internship at NASA. Which estimates your location by measuring the signal strength (RSSI) from nearby WiFi access points. By comparing these signal strengths to known positions, using trilateration, you cancalculate your approximate location within the network's coverage area.
