I'm Mark.

A junior studying majoring in Computer Science, and minoring in Human Development at the University Of Maryland and a current Software Engineer Intern at NASA.

At NASA, I'm working on developing an RSSI (Received Signal Strength Indicator) positioning system using C. The project involves creating a reliable method for determining the location of devices based on the strength of their Wi-Fi signals. It's a super exciting challenge that blends my skills in software development and my interest in solving real-world problems with technology.

Outside of my tech pursuits, I'm all about staying active. You'll find me in the gym, playing basketball, getting into a heated game of table tennis, or exploring nature on a good hike.
